Logic simulator

This is a circuit simulator made in Java with the JavaFX framework. Build whatever you want in this playground, even your own 8-bit pc.

Try it right now Source code
There are many types of blocks available like AND and NOT gates, Switches, Lights and RGB Lights, 7-digit Display, Bus, Tri-state Buffer and circuit embedding by using chips.

Available for Linux (arm64 and amd64), Windows (executable and installer), MacOS and even for the browser.

Retoohs

Retoohs is a reversed shooter game where you are the zombies and you have to kill the player and avoid letting them get points.

An entry for the GMTK game jam 2023.

Try it right now Source code
Retoohs (reversed name of shooter) is a game where you switch roles every 20s, when you are the player you need to kill the zombies and collect the blue points; don't get too OP because when you play as the zombies you need to defeat yourself and avoid letting the player to score points (try to collect the drops and to dodge the player's bullets). At 1500 you meet your first boss.

Available for Linux (amd64), Windows (executable and installer), MacOS, android and even for the browser.

Chess

Chess GUI made in JavaFX. Supports singleplayer and multiplayer.

Try it right now Source code
A chess client that supports playing against stockfish or against your friend in LAN or on the dedicated server. You can also export the game to a PGN or load a custom one. Custom time control available too.

Available for Linux (arm64 and amd64), Windows (executable and installer), MacOS, android and even for the browser.

Food Dice

Food Dice is a very simple platformer where you play as a dice and every couple of seconds you get a special effect that may help you or may not. An entry for the GMTK game jam 2022

Try it right now Source code
Food Dice is a food platformer where you are a dice and every 15 seconds, based on which face you land on, you get special effects. You have to reach the end without getting into lasers or shooters.

Available for Linux (arm64 and amd64), Windows (executable and installer), MacOS, android and even for the browser.
